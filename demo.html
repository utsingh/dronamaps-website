  • About the Project:

    The project of 3D Mapping of the Industrial Area awarded to DronaMaps by the state organizations MPSEDC & MAP_IT, started with the ambitious goal to eliminate the need for investors to visit any area before making a decision to invest. We are glad to have completed the project successfully and are now working on creating a repository of 3D Data, which can be used by the authorities for purposes not limiting to:

    1. Promote the industrial areas in a very innovative manner and attract global investors

    2. Track growth in area of development

    3. Plan large scale events with the help of scientific information
